Voxel Mars

A global-scale reproduction of Mars in voxel.js, using topographic data from MOLA.

Controls:

  • WASD to move
  • [SPACE] to jump
  • click to lock cursor
  • [ESC] to unlock cursor
  • C to toggle camera perspective
  • M to toggle global map
  • P to pause and resume

Source: http://github.com/rachel-carvalho/voxel-mars
