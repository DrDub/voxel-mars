Voxel Mars

A global-scale reproduction of Mars in voxel.js, using topographic data from MOLA.

Controls:

  • Click world screen to control
  • ESC to release
  • W A S D to move
  • SPACE to jump
  • C to toggle camera perspective
  • M to toggle global map
  • P to pause and see this screen

About/source: http://github.com/rachel-carvalho/voxel-mars
