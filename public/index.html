Voxel Mars

A global-scale reproduction of the red planet in voxel.js, using topographic data from MOLA.

  • Click world screen to control
  • ESC to release
  • W A S D to move
  • SPACE to jump
  • C to toggle camera perspective
  • M to toggle global map
  • P to pause and see this screen
